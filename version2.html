About Me

I'm Timothy Rollings, a forever learner who loves combinging mathematics, data science, and machine learning to solve problems. My love for mathematics, technology, and exploring new ideas has exposed me to many fields and experiences through the years.

I've been fortunate enough to work with amazing engineers, scientists, and businesses within start ups, engineering firms, oil companies, and research institutions. These experiences have only further inspired me to explore new ways in solving problems through mathematics and data science.

I'm always looking for new ways to solve real-world problems by fusing data with mathematics. If I'm not solving problems with others, I'm on AppliedMath.org helping others learn the beauty of mathematics and its applications.
