About Me

I'm Timothy Rollings, a forever learner who loves combining computer science, mathematics, and machine learning to solve problems. My love for computer science, mathematics, and exploring new ideas has exposed me to many fields and experiences over the years.

I've been fortunate enough to work with amazing engineers, scientists, and businessmen within start-ups, engineering firms, oil companies, and research institutions. These experiences have only further inspired me to explore new ways of solving problems through mathematics and data science.

I'm always looking for new ways to solve real-world problems by fusing data with computer science and mathematics principles. If I'm not solving problems with others, I'm building MathApplied.org to help others learn mathematics and its use within fields like machine learning, computer science, and more.

More About Me

Computer Science and Mathematics Education

Seeing the impact computer science and mathematics has had on my life and career, I love sharing how they are used in different fields. MathApplied.org is a recent ongoing development of mine in doing just this.

I've helped oil pipelines across North America looking to understand complex fluid dynamics by combining mathematics, data science, and machine learning.

Swarm Intelligence Research

I've researched particle swarm optimization algorithms and their use in swarm intelligence applications in complex environments thanks to funding by the National Science Foundation.
